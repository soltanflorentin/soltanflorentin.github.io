About the practice

A career dedicated to good lawyering.

Maria Popescu has practiced in the Bucharest Bar since 2015, providing legal counsel and court representation for individuals, entrepreneurs and companies. The practice is defined by a rigorous approach, direct communication and respect for the client's time.

Professional
photograph

I became a lawyer because, even in law school, I believed that the law is not merely a set of norms — it is an instrument that, used with skill and honesty, can change the trajectory of a life, save a business or bring peace to a family.

I graduated from the Faculty of Law at the University of Bucharest in 2014 and joined the Bucharest Bar the following year. Since then, I have built a practice grounded in three principles: deep understanding of each case, clear strategy explained in terms the client can grasp, and firmness in defending their interests — at the negotiation table or in court.

I work with Romanian clients and with foreign residents, providing counsel in both Romanian and English. The practice partners with a solid network of professionals — notaries, bailiffs, technical and tax experts — to deliver complete solutions wherever a case extends beyond purely legal questions.

I believe a quality consultation starts with a good question and ends with a client who understands exactly where they stand, what their options are and what comes next. That is what I aim for, regardless of the file's complexity.
