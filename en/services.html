Practice areas

Legal services from the local court to Strasbourg.

The practice covers both specialized representation before the European Court of Human Rights and the principal branches of domestic law. Below is a closer look at each area.

01
European Human Rights Law

Representation before the Court in Strasbourg

Specialized assistance for litigants who have exhausted their domestic remedies. I assess the admissibility criteria, draft the application, and represent you before the European Court of Human Rights, with a focus on structural violations of the Convention.

  • Counsel on violations of fundamental rights
  • Assessment of ECtHR admissibility criteria
  • Drafting and filing of applications
  • Representation before the ECtHR (Strasbourg)
  • Right to a fair trial (Art. 6)
  • Protection of property (Art. 1, Protocol No. 1)
  • Prohibition of inhuman treatment (Art. 3)
  • Obtaining just satisfaction
02
Civil Law

Property rights and obligations

Counsel and representation across civil law relations, with an emphasis on property rights and the enforcement of final court decisions.

  • Civil law counsel
  • Representation in civil disputes
  • Drafting notices and statements of claim
  • Drafting contracts and settlements
  • Actions concerning property rights
  • Legalization and enforcement of court decisions
  • Drafting challenges
  • Certification of documents
03
Criminal Law

Defense in criminal proceedings

Assistance and representation before criminal investigation bodies and courts of every level, with firm defense of procedural rights.

  • Criminal law counsel
  • Assistance before criminal investigation bodies
  • Representation before courts of every level
  • Drafting criminal complaints
  • Challenges against measures ordered by the prosecutor
04
Family Law

Balance in a sensitive field

Counsel and representation in family law disputes, protecting the best interests of the child and preserving dialogue between the parties wherever possible.

  • Family law counsel
  • Drafting applications and court representation
  • Divorce
  • Division of marital property
  • Child support and maintenance
  • Child custody and parental authority
  • Visitation schedules
  • Contesting / establishing paternity
05
Employment Law

Fair employment relations

Counsel and representation for employees and employers before the competent courts and the empowered public institutions.

  • Employment law counsel
  • Representation in labor disputes
  • Counsel on employment contracts
  • Drafting contracts, agreements and notices
  • Legalization and enforcement of court decisions
06
Administrative Litigation

Dialogue with state authorities

Challenging unlawful administrative acts and representation before the courts competent to resolve administrative litigation disputes.

  • Administrative litigation counsel
  • Representation in administrative litigation disputes
  • Drafting actions to annul administrative acts
07
Mediation

Amicable solutions

Mediation services across every branch of law, to resolve disputes through dialogue without resorting to the courts.

  • Mediation across every branch of law
  • Facilitating dialogue between the parties
  • Drafting the mediation agreement
Working with me

Professional ethics and confidentiality

Fees

Fee policy

Fees are set according to the complexity of the case, by prior agreement with the client.

Fixed (flat) fees

Agreed for clearly defined projects or for distinct procedural stages.

Hourly fees

For matters requiring ongoing counsel, extensive document review or lengthy negotiations.

Success fees

A complementary option, agreed exclusively within the limits of the law, directly proportional to the favorable result obtained.
