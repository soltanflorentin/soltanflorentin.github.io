Practice areas

Legal services with broad coverage, delivered with rigor.

The practice covers the principal branches of private law and several areas of public law, providing both preventive counsel and representation in disputes. Below is a closer look at each service area.

01
Civil Law

Property rights and obligations

Counsel and representation across civil law relations: contracts, property, inheritance, partitions, tort and contractual liability. Both preventive counsel and court action, with emphasis on amicable resolution wherever possible.

  • Drafting and negotiating civil contracts
  • Property reclamation and boundary actions
  • Inheritance and certificates of heir
  • Judicial and voluntary partitions
  • Tort liability
  • Adverse possession and acquisitive prescription
  • Declaratory and enforcement actions
  • Enforcement proceedings — objections and challenges
02
Family Law

Balance in a sensitive field

Family matters require both legal competence and empathy. The practice provides discreet counsel in divorce, partition, parental authority and child protection — aiming to preserve dialogue between the parties and protect children's interests.

  • Divorce — notarial, administrative or judicial
  • Division of marital property
  • Parental authority
  • Establishment of child's residence
  • Visitation schedules
  • Child support
  • Protection orders
  • Adoption and paternity actions
03
Commercial & Corporate Law

Partners for your business

Legal counsel throughout a company's lifecycle: incorporation, operations, restructuring, dissolution. The practice serves both early-stage entrepreneurs and established companies, with preventive solutions that reduce legal risk and long-term cost.

  • Company formation (LLC, JSC, sole proprietorships)
  • Trade Registry filings
  • Drafting commercial contracts
  • Negotiations and due diligence
  • Debt recovery — orders to pay, enforcement
  • Insolvency and judicial reorganization
  • Commercial litigation
  • GDPR and compliance
04
Real Estate Law

Real estate transactions, safely

Careful review of a real estate transaction can make the difference between a sound investment and years of litigation. Full due diligence, contract drafting and closing support — for buyers, sellers, developers and investors.

  • Real estate due diligence
  • Sale & purchase agreements
  • Preliminary contracts
  • Lease agreements
  • Land registry filings, partitions, consolidations
  • Building permit challenges
  • Real estate disputes
  • Closing assistance at the notary
05
Employment Law

Fair employment relations

Counsel for employees and employers across the entire employment relationship, including disputes. Both preventive work (compliance audits, drafting contracts and internal rules) and litigation — contesting dismissals and recovering wages.

  • Individual and collective labor contracts
  • Internal regulations and job descriptions
  • Challenges to termination
  • Recovery of wages
  • Workplace harassment and discrimination
  • Disciplinary investigations
  • Employee liability
  • Labor court litigation
06
Administrative Law

Dialogue with state authorities

Challenging unlawful administrative acts, support in authorization procedures and representation in disputes with public authorities. The practice works with individuals and with companies that interact frequently with the administration.

  • Challenging administrative acts
  • Preliminary complaints
  • Administrative court procedure
  • Building permits and zoning
  • Public procurement — challenges
  • Tax disputes
  • Public authority liability
  • Citizenship, residence, permits
