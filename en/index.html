About the practice

A cross-border practice, dedicated to fundamental rights.

My principal specialization is European human rights law and international litigation. The practice focuses on representing citizens before the European Court of Human Rights (ECtHR) in Strasbourg.

I offer a final, firm line of defense against legislative, administrative or judicial abuses by state institutions — as well as counsel in civil, criminal, employment, family and administrative law. The mission stays the same: restoring legality and obtaining just redress.
